# Profile

My name is Federico Mora Rocha. I am currently an applied scientist at [Amazon Web Services](https://www.amazon.science/research-areas/automated-reasoning). Starting fall 2026, I will be an assistant professor of computer science at the [University of Waterloo](https://cs.uwaterloo.ca/) and a faculty affiliate at the [Vector Institute](https://vectorinstitute.ai/).

I study automated reasoning, programming language theory, and neuro-symbolic artificial intelligence. For more information about my work, see my [curriculum vitae](cv.html).
